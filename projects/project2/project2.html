Context Contexte Contexto

The topic of this project is based on the growing importance of crime as a social issue affecting many cities around the world. In my home country, Peru, I have observed how crime has become increasingly violent over the years. Similarly, Montréal has also experienced a rise in criminal activity in recent years. Due to the availability and accessibility of open-source data, this study was conducted using residential burglary data from the city of Boston between 2015 and 2022. I also chose Boston because I previously lived there, which helped me better understand the urban context and interpret the results of the analysis. This project demonstrates how spatial-temporal analysis methods can be used to study crime patterns.
