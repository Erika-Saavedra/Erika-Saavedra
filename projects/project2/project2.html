Context Contexte Contexto

In my home country, Peru, I have observed how crime has become increasingly violent over the years. Similarly, Montréal has also experienced a rise in criminal activity in recent years. Due to the availability and accessibility of open-source data, this study was conducted using residential burglary data from the city of Boston between 2015 and 2022. I also chose Boston because I previously lived there, which helped me better understand the urban context and interpret the results of the analysis. Objectives Objetifs Objetivos

This project aims to identify the spatial and temporal trends of residential burglaries in the city of Boston between 2015 and 2022 using geospatial data and the R programming language. This analysis of crime concentrations will help better understand their evolution over time and support preventive management and decision-making. Ce projet vise à identifier les tendances spatiales et temporelles des cambriolages résidentiels dans la ville de Boston entre 2015 et 2022 à l’aide de données géospatiales et du langage de programmation R. Cette analyse des concentrations criminelles permettra de mieux comprendre leur évolution dans le temps et de soutenir la gestion préventive ainsi que la prise de décision. Este proyecto tiene como objetivo identificar las tendencias espaciales y temporales de los robos residenciales en la ciudad de Boston entre 2015 y 2022 utilizando datos geoespaciales y el lenguaje de programación R. Este análisis de las concentraciones delictivas permitirá comprender mejor su evolución en el tiempo y apoyar la gestión preventiva y la toma de decisiones.

Conclusions Conclusions Conclusiones

The cartographic and statistical results highlight a higher concentration of residential burglaries during weekdays, particularly on Tuesdays and Fridays, between 4 p.m. and 6 p.m., as well as around midnight, mainly in the neighborhoods of Downtown, Allston, South End, Dorchester, and Roxbury. Les résultats cartographiques et statistiques mettent en évidence une concentration plus importante des cambriolages résidentiels en semaine, notamment les mardis et vendredis, entre 16 h et 18 h ainsi qu’autour de minuit, principalement dans les quartiers de Downtown, Allston, South End, Dorchester et Roxbury. Los resultados cartográficos y estadísticos evidencian una mayor concentración de robos residenciales durante los días laborables, particularmente los martes y viernes, entre las 16:00 y 18:00 horas, así como alrededor de la medianoche, principalmente en los barrios de Downtown, Allston, South End, Dorchester y Roxbury.
